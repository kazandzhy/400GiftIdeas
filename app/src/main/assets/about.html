Hello, my name is Vlad Kazandzhy, and I am the developer of this Android application.

This application is not intended to show all possible gift ideas. There are so many things and inventions in the world today, and their number will only increase in the future. Also, the application does not show specific gifts, but rather directs users to websites where they can choose something based on their personal preferences. The main purpose of this project is to offer a variety of ideas for gifts, and after that you more than welcome to come up with something of your own.

In the application, you can find ideas for gifts according to the age of the person, life interests, and gender. You can also find ideas for a special event, and in the section Extras, there are additional gift ideas that are suitable for everyone.

In the top right menu, you can try the Random Gift function if you'd like to randomly browse the 400+ gift ideas. Also, on the Contact Us page, you can send your own gift ideas, a new gift category, or a gift website that you enjoy, or you can share your opinion or ideas about the app.
